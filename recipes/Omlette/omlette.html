Omlette Recipe:


A fried mixed egg

Not much to say here, you take and egg, you brake it, you have an omlette

You can add stuff to it if you want, like cheese or veggies, but its ok plain as well

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Canola oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Other stuff if you like

Steps:

  1. Crack the egg into a bowl
  2. Mix the egg in the bowl and add seasoning
  3. Heat up a pan with oil
  4. Pour the egg mix onto the hot pan
  5. Flip over from time to time until done
